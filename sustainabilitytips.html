Tip 1: Opt for Plant-Based Diets

Incorporating more plant-based foods into your meals reduces the demand for meat which lowers greenhouse gas emissions and decreases deforestation caused by agricultural expansion.

Tip 2: Energy Efficiency at Home

Make simple changes like using natural light during the day, wearing appropriate clothing to reduce heating and cooling needs, and investing in energy-efficient appliances.

Tip 3: Use Public Transport

Instead of driving, use public transportation, carpool, or bike which can significantly reduce your carbon emissions and decrease air pollution in your community.

Tip 4: Waste Reduction

Aim to produce less waste by choosing products with minimal packaging, reusing items, and recycling materials properly. The 3 R's to remember this are Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle.

Tip 5: Support Local Produce

Buying fruits, vegetables, and other food items from local farmers reduces the carbon footprint associated with long-distance transportation and supports local economies.
